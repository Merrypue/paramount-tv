Sometimes, you may encounter error messages such as “Invalid Login” or “We couldn’t find an account with that email.” This could be due to an incorrect username, expired credentials, or issues with your subscription. If you have an active subscription but still cannot access content, your payment method may have failed, or there could be an issue with your billing cycle. Check your payment status and ensure that there are no issues with your subscription.

If you are using a smart TV or another device and facing issues logging in, make sure the app is up to date. Additionally, try restarting the device or uninstalling and reinstalling the app to resolve minor glitches.
